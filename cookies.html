Cookie Policy

How this website uses browser storage, cookies, and similar technologies.

Last updated: April 19, 2026

This policy should be updated if analytics, advertising pixels, embedded maps, third-party chat, or external AI services are added.

1. What Cookies Are

Cookies are small files stored by your browser. Similar technologies include local storage, session storage, pixels, and scripts that remember preferences or help understand website usage.

2. Current Website Storage

The current website uses local storage to remember your day/night mode preference. This improves your browsing experience and does not require an account.

3. Chatbot Storage

The current chatbot is a local scripted assistant. It does not currently store chatbot conversations in a backend or send them to an external AI provider. If this changes, the Privacy Policy and this Cookie Policy should be updated.

4. Analytics and Third-Party Cookies

At this stage, the website codebase does not include analytics or advertising pixels. If tools such as Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, embedded videos, maps, or third-party chat widgets are added later, they may set their own cookies.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. You can also clear local storage to reset the website theme preference.

6. Contact

Questions about cookies can be sent to hello@triaxis.tech.
