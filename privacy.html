Privacy Policy

How TriAxis Technologies handles personal information shared through our website, contact form, and chatbot.

Last updated: April 19, 2026

This is a practical starter policy for the website. It should be reviewed by a qualified legal professional before being relied on as formal legal advice.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information you choose to provide, including your name, email address, phone number, company details, project requirements, and any message you submit through the contact form or chatbot.

We may also receive basic technical information such as browser type, device type, pages visited, referring links, and approximate location derived from standard web logs or analytics tools if enabled.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to respond to enquiries, prepare proposals, provide requested services, improve the website experience, protect the website from misuse, and maintain business records.

3. Contact Form and Chatbot

The contact form currently displays a frontend confirmation and does not connect to a production backend in this codebase. If a backend or email service is connected later, submitted messages may be sent to TriAxis Technologies for follow-up.

The chatbot currently runs as a scripted browser-based assistant. It does not send messages to Hugging Face, OpenAI, or another AI provider in the current implementation. If a third-party AI service is added later, this policy should be updated to explain what data is shared and why.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

We use local storage to remember your day/night theme preference. If analytics, advertising, or embedded third-party services are added later, they may use cookies or similar technologies as described in our Cookie Policy.

5. Sharing Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers who help us operate the website, respond to enquiries, host systems, or deliver services, provided they are expected to protect that information appropriately.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. However, no website, email, or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

7. Your Choices

You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of personal information you have provided, subject to legal and business record requirements.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at hello@triaxis.tech.
