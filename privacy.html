Privacy Policy

How TriAxis Technologies handles personal information shared through our website, contact form, and chatbot.

Last updated: April 19, 2026

This is a practical starter policy for the website. It should be reviewed by a qualified legal professional before being relied on as formal legal advice.

1. Who We Are

TriAxis Technologies provides software development, cloud, cybersecurity, data, consulting, and managed IT support services. This policy explains how we handle information collected through this website.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information you choose to provide when you contact us or interact with the website, including:

  • Name, email address, phone number, company name, and role.
  • Project details, service interests, timelines, budgets, and messages submitted through forms.
  • Chatbot messages or prompts you type into the website assistant.
  • Basic technical information such as browser type, device type, referring page, and pages visited if analytics or web logs are enabled.

3. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Respond to enquiries and schedule discovery calls.
  • Prepare proposals, estimates, and project recommendations.
  • Provide, improve, and personalize our website and services.
  • Maintain business records and protect the website from misuse.
  • Comply with legal, security, accounting, or operational requirements.

4. Contact Form

The contact form currently displays a frontend confirmation in this codebase. If a backend, email service, CRM, or form handling provider is connected later, submitted information may be sent to TriAxis Technologies and relevant service providers so we can respond to you.

5. Chatbot

The current chatbot is a scripted browser-based assistant. It does not send messages to Hugging Face, OpenAI, or another AI provider in the current implementation. It is designed to answer basic questions about TriAxis services, pricing, contact details, and getting started.

If we later connect the chatbot to Hugging Face or another third-party AI service, user messages may be processed by that provider. This policy should be updated before that feature goes live.

6. Cookies and Local Storage

We use local storage to remember your day/night theme preference. If analytics, advertising, embedded media, maps, or third-party chatbot tools are added later, they may use cookies or similar technologies. See our Cookie Policy for more detail.

7. Sharing Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers that help us host the website, operate forms, manage communications, provide security, or deliver client services. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, users, systems, or business.

8. Data Retention

We keep enquiry and project information only for as long as reasonably needed to respond, maintain business records, provide services, resolve disputes, or meet legal obligations. If you ask us to delete information, we will review the request and respond subject to any lawful retention needs.

9. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. However, no website, email, network, or internet transmission is completely secure, so you should avoid submitting highly sensitive information through the website.

10. Your Choices

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may be able to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or a copy of personal information you have provided. You may also object to certain processing or withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

11. Children's Privacy

This website is intended for business users and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our website, chatbot, analytics, or service processes change. The updated date at the top of this page will show when it was last revised.

13. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact us at hello@triaxis.tech.
